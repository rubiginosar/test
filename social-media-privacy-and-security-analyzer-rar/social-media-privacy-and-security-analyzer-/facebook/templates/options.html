Activate Two-Factor Authentication

Enhance your account security by enabling 2FA. This adds an extra layer of protection by requiring a verification code in addition
to your password.

Create a Strong Password

Ensure the security of your account by using a robust password. Combine uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters for better protection.

Update Passwords Regularly

Periodically changing your passwords helps prevent unauthorized access. Avoid using the same password across multiple
platforms to enhance security.

Beware of Phishing Attempts

Stay vigilant against phishing attacks. Avoid clicking on suspicious links and never share sensitive information in response to unsolicited messages.
